About the Artist

Hailing originally from the heart of Virginia, Josh Starkey has brought his unique blend of Americana and folk to the vibrant music scene of Wilmington, North Carolina. With a sound that is both timeless and fresh, he captivates audiences with a mix of heartfelt original songs and thoughtfully chosen covers. Josh's music tells stories of life, love, and the journey in between, creating an authentic and memorable experience for every listener.
